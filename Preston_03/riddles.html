Bio Education Riddles

Riddles

1. Poor people have me, rich people need me, if you eat me, you die. What am I?

2. What two words, added together, contain the most letters?

3. What four-letter word can be written forward, backword, or upside down, and can still be read from left to right?

4. What time is it when an elephant sits on a fence?

5. What can go up a chimney down, but can’t go down a chimney up?

6. What can you hold in your right hand, but never in your left hand?

7. What can you catch, but not throw?

8. What has many teeth, but cannot bite?

9. What has lots of eyes, but can’t see?

10. What can travel all around the world without leaving its corner?
