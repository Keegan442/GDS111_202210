My Bio

Whats up! I'm Keegan Preston and I'm from Wakefield, Rhode Island. I like a whole bunch of stuff! I like skating, games, movies, art, music, and a bunch more! One of my favorite hobbies, though, is skateboarding. I started skating when I was ten years old and fell in love with it. I took a couple year break, then came back to it and was more into it than ever. Now, I can do a bunch of tricks and am very into the culture of skating.
Another hobby that I'm very into is music. One of my favorite artists of all time is MF DOOM. He started making music when he was younger in a group called KMD with his brother. After the death of his brother, he took a break, but then came back in 1999 with his first solo studio album called "Operation: Doomsday" and everyone was stunned with his unique production style and innovative rap style. After that he kept topping himself with album after album becomming better and better.
